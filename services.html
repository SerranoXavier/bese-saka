Services

At Bese Saka Store, we offer many services to our customers in order to meet everybody’s needs. Bese Saka’s team is always ready and glad to help each and everyone.

Advice

Everyone at Bese Saka Store is committed to give strong, competent and wise advice to anyone who asks, all of that with a big smile!

Click and Collect

Give us your shopping list and within 2 hours just come to Bese Saka Store, and your groceries will be ready!

Home delivery

You cannot come to Bese Saka Store but really need that particular item? No panic! Just give us your shopping list and within 4 hours, your groceries will be delivered right into your kitchen!

Keep in mind that additional fees will be charged, depending on the delivering location ad that we will not be able to deliver outside a area bigger than 20 kilometers around Bese Saka Store.
